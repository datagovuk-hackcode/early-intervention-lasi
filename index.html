Certain issues have legs...

  • Many social issues originate in one Local Authority and impact others
  • A typical case of this is homelessness, as people in need leave to explore wealthier towns and areas of the UK looking for shelter and opportunities
  • This is a challenge for early social intervention, as there isn't a platform where LA Commissioners can exchange data illustrating these issues and orchestrate planning together
  • ... well, until today...

Enter "L.A.S.I."

  • LASI is a platform for censing any intelligence / data all LA Commissioners in the UK have available about issues that "move", such as homelessness
  • Data is collected and consolidated in an unified view that becomes a platform for joint planning across borders
  • Information is cross-
